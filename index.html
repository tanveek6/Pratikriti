why choose us??

Pratikriti is a website that specializes in creating customized, hand-drawn portraits of individuals and pets. These mini portraits are incredibly unique and personal, making for a perfect gift or keepsake. The website offers a range of options, including portrait size, medium (pen or pencil), and background color. Customers can simply upload a photo of their subject and provide any special instructions or preferences. The talented artists behind Mini Portraits will then create a beautiful, one-of-a-kind portrait that captures the essence of the individual or pet. The website also offers a gallery of past work for inspiration and to showcase their impressive talents. Overall, Mini Portraits provides a personalized, high-quality service that brings joy and sentimental value to its customers.
